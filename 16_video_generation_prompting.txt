16 — VIDEO GENERATION MODEL PROMPTING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

MODELS: Sora, Runway Gen-3, Pika, Kling

─────────────────────────────────────────
VIDEO PROMPT ADDITIONS OVER IMAGE
─────────────────────────────────────────

Motion      → how the subject moves.
Camera move → pan, tilt, dolly, zoom, steadicam, drone, pull-back reveal.
Duration    → 5 seconds, 10 seconds.
Transition  → how scenes connect (cut, fade, dissolve).

─────────────────────────────────────────
CAMERA MOVEMENTS
─────────────────────────────────────────

Pan          → rotate left/right on fixed axis.
Tilt         → rotate up/down on fixed axis.
Dolly/Track  → camera physically moves through space.
Zoom         → focal length changes (not same as dolly).
Steadicam    → smooth movement while walking.
Aerial/Drone → bird's eye view.
Pull-back    → starts close, reveals context as it pulls out.

─────────────────────────────────────────
NEGATIVE PROMPTS FOR VIDEO
─────────────────────────────────────────

fast cuts, text overlays, watermark, flickering,
digital artifacts, morphing faces, jump cuts, logos.

─────────────────────────────────────────
CONTINUITY RULE
─────────────────────────────────────────

For multi-scene videos: maintain consistent lighting, color palette, camera position.
Label scenes [Scene 1], [Scene 2], [Scene 3] and use same style descriptor each time.
