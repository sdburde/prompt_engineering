06 — CHAIN OF THOUGHT (CoT) PROMPTING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

─────────────────────────────────────────
KEYWORDS
─────────────────────────────────────────

CoT            → Make the model show reasoning steps before the final answer.
                 Improves accuracy on math, logic, multi-step tasks.

Zero-shot CoT  → Append "Let's think step by step." to any question.
                 Kojima et al. 2022. No examples needed.

Few-shot CoT   → Provide examples WITH reasoning steps.
                 Problem: ... Reasoning: ... Answer: N
                 More reliable than zero-shot for complex tasks.

─────────────────────────────────────────
WHY CoT WORKS
─────────────────────────────────────────

Forces model to allocate compute to intermediate steps.
Each step conditions the next — errors caught earlier.
Reasoning trace is auditable by humans.

─────────────────────────────────────────
PATTERNS
─────────────────────────────────────────

Zero-shot:   "{problem}\n\nThink step by step."
Few-shot:    Show 2–3 (problem, reasoning, answer) triples, then new problem.
Structured:  "Step 1: ... Step 2: ... Step 3: ... Final answer:"

─────────────────────────────────────────
WHEN TO USE
─────────────────────────────────────────

Math word problems, logic puzzles, multi-step coding.
NOT needed for simple classification or retrieval.
Use temperature=0 for CoT to get deterministic reasoning.
