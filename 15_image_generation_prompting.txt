15 — IMAGE GENERATION MODEL PROMPTING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

MODELS: DALL-E 3, Stable Diffusion, Midjourney, Flux

─────────────────────────────────────────
PROMPT ANATOMY
─────────────────────────────────────────

Subject    → what/who is in the image.
Style      → art style, medium, artist reference.
Composition → framing, camera angle, rule of thirds.
Lighting   → golden hour, studio, backlit, dramatic.
Quality    → 8k, highly detailed, trending on artstation.

─────────────────────────────────────────
KEYWORDS
─────────────────────────────────────────

Negative prompt  → Terms the model should AVOID.
                   blurry, distorted, extra limbs, watermark, text, low quality.

Style transfer   → Add artist or movement name: "in the style of Monet", "cyberpunk".

Aspect ratio     → 16:9 (landscape), 1:1 (square), 9:16 (portrait).

─────────────────────────────────────────
COMPOSITION TERMS
─────────────────────────────────────────

rule of thirds, centered, symmetrical, close-up,
bird's eye view, worm's eye view, bokeh background,
wide shot, medium shot, portrait

─────────────────────────────────────────
LLM REFINEMENT PATTERN
─────────────────────────────────────────

Prompt LLM: "Improve this image prompt: '{weak}'. Add style, lighting, composition, quality terms. Under 100 words."
Use the improved prompt with the image gen model.
